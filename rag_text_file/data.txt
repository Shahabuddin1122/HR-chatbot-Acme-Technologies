What are HR Policies?,
HR policies are\nguidelines that companies set for employee relations in the workplace. These\nstandardized rules ensure consistency by defining acceptable behavior,\noutlining employee expectations, detailing disciplinary procedures, and\nspecifying organizational responsibilities. Companies implement these policies\nto demonstrate their commitment to regulatory compliance, minimum employment\nstandards, training, diversity, and ethical practices.,
